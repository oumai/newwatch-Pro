①Button

1.Power on/off

Long press to power on/off the watch.

2.Enter the medical examination interface

Short press to enter the medical examination interface.

3.Return to the main interface

In the conditions except for when the second- or third-level setting menus are displayed,press to return to the main interface.


②Button

1.Call the preset emergency number

Press and hold for 3 seconds to call the preset emercy number,and open the GPS after call hang up.


③Button

1.Activate the voice time teller

Quickly press twice to activate the voice time teller(Can be set up to open or close the voice time teller)。


④Button

Call the next affection number

Press and hold for 3 seconds to call the family numbers。
