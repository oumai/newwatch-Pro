①Button

1.Power on/off

Press and hold for 3 seconds to switch on/off the watch.

2.Enter the medic

When the main interface is displayed, press and hold.

3.Increase the volume

During a call, you can press to increase the volume.

4.Move up

When setting menus are displayed, press the button to move up.

5.Activate the time teller

Press the button twice to tell the current time.

6.Back button

It acts as a “Back” button as well except you’re in the setting mode.


②Button

1.SOS button

Press and hold for 3 seconds to call the pre-set emergency number.

2.End call

Press the button to hang up the call.


③Button

1.Decrease the volume

You are able to decrease volume during a call.

2.Move down

When setting menus are displayed, press the button to move down.

3.“Family Number” button

Press and hold for 3 seconds to call the pre-set family number.

4.Phonebook

Press the button twice to display the phonebook.


①+③Button

Press the buttons ① and ③ simultaneously.
