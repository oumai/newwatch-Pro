How to Register

1. Register & Login

Register: New user need to register a new account in order to use the apps. First, enter the phone number for registration, click on the code and your phone will receive a verification code. Enter the verification code.
Login: Login using your registered username and password.

2. Add Device

Click the upper right corner of the Device Manager. Enter the IMEI number, authentication code and the basic information. Click “Save” to successfully add the device.

3. Add family number

In the Device Manager, select the device that you want to add and click “Edit”. After that click the “Dial-up Settings” and enter your number.

Home

1. Getting to know about the wearer’s health information

Click “Health” and you are able to view your health information.

2. Getting to know about the wearer’s location

Click “Location” and you are able to view the history of the location information.

3. Authentication

Click “Member Services” and you will be able to enter the authentication page.

4. Call

Click “Call” and choose the device that you want to call, it will directly call the wearer which will incur charges.

Location

1. Location Records

Press the button on top right button to select wearer. You are able to view the selected wearer’s history records, SOS records.

Health

1. Health Records

Press on the top right button to select wearer. You are able to view the selected wearer’s health parameters information.

2. History of Health data

Press the button below to choose the date that you wish to view.

Sidebar

1. Account

Click to enter your personal information.

2. Device

2.1 Add a device
Click on the top right icon, enter the corresponding information to connect your device to your account.
2.2 Edit a device
Select on one of the devices that you wish to make changes, select “Edit” to modify and select “Save” to keep the changes.
